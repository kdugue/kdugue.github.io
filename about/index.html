About

Kemet is a self-taught Haitian-American software developer. He currently works at Uber. Previously, he worked on the Teams product at Microsoft. And before that, he worked at SelfMade.

When he's not working, he likes to play tennis, listen to lo-fi house, read short fiction, do yoga, and think of ways to live a more minimalist life.
